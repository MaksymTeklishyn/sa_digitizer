TVector3 ChargeCarrierTransport::move ( ChargeCarrier &  particle)

Moves the charge carrier by one time step considering drift, diffusion, and the Lorentz force.

This method calculates the total displacement of the charge carrier due to diffusion, drift in the electric field, and the effect of the magnetic field. The particle's position is then updated based on this displacement.
